Why semantic HTML wins

When you embrace classless frameworks, you focus on structure, not presentational clutter. PicoCSS styles <section>, <article>, buttons, and forms intelligently. You get consistent spacing, accessible interactive states, and native form styles.

📦 Lightweight (~10kb) ♿ ARIA ready 🎯 Customizable via CSS vars
